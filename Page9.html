8. KNOWLEDGE

Knowledge is the awareness, understanding, and information gained through experience, education, or study.
It involves the comprehension of facts, truths, or principles in a particular area or subject. Knowledge is important because it enables informed decision-making, problem-solving, and innovation.
It empowers individuals to understand the world, adapt to changes, and contribute effectively to their personal and professional lives.

Quotes

Here are five quotes about knowledge:
1. "Knowledge is power." — Francis Bacon
2. "An investment in knowledge pays the best interest." — Benjamin Franklin
3. "To know, is to know that you know nothing. That is the meaning of true knowledge." — Socrates
4. "The only true wisdom is in knowing you know nothing." — Socrates
5. "Knowledge, like air, is vital to life. Like air, no one should be denied it." — Alan Moore
