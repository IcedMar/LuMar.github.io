9. WISDOM

Wisdom is the ability to make sound judgments and decisions based on knowledge, experience, and understanding.
It involves insight, foresight, and the application of learned principles to real-life situations.
Wisdom is valuable because it helps individuals navigate complex and uncertain situations, promotes ethical and thoughtful behavior, and contributes to personal well-being and the common good.
It balances knowledge with compassion and foresight, guiding actions that benefit both the individual and society.

Quotes

Here are five quotes about wisdom:
1. "The only true wisdom is in knowing you know nothing." — Socrates
2. "Turn your wounds into wisdom." — Oprah Winfrey
3. "Wisdom is not a product of schooling but of the lifelong attempt to acquire it." — Albert Einstein
4. "Knowledge speaks, but wisdom listens." — Jimi Hendrix
5. "The fool do think he is wise, but the wise man knows himself to be a fool." — William Shakespeare
