10. INFORMATION

Information is data that has been processed, organized, or structured in a way that makes it meaningful and useful.
It can come in various forms, such as facts, figures, instructions, or news. Information is important because it facilitates communication, supports decision-making, enhances knowledge, and drives innovation.
It enables individuals and organizations to understand contexts, identify opportunities and challenges, and respond effectively to changes and developments.

Quotes

Here are five quotes about information:
1. "Information is the oil of the 21st century, and analytics is the combustion engine." — Peter Sondergaard
2. "In a world of information, ignorance is a choice." — Donny Miller
3. "Information is not knowledge." — Albert Einstein
4. "Information is the resolution of uncertainty." — Claude Shannon
5. "The most important thing in communication is hearing what isn't said." — Peter Drucker
