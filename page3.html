2. ANIMALS

Animals are multicellular organisms belonging to the kingdom Animalia.
They are characterized by their ability to move spontaneously and independently, ingest food to obtain energy, and often possess sensory and nervous systems for responding to their environment.
Animals play crucial roles in ecosystems as consumers, prey, and predators, contributing to biodiversity and ecological balance.
They also provide humans with food, companionship, transportation, and have been essential in scientific research and understanding biological processes.
Overall, animals are integral to the functioning of ecosystems and hold significant cultural, ecological, and economic importance worldwide.

Quotes

Here are five notable quotes about animals:
1. "The greatness of a nation and its moral progress can be judged by the way its animals are treated." - Mahatma Gandhi
2. "Until one has loved an animal, a part of one's soul remains unawakened." - Anatole France
3. "Animals are such agreeable friends - they ask no questions; they pass no criticisms." - George Eliot
4. "The love for all living creatures is the most noble attribute of man." - Charles Darwin
5. "The greatness of a community is most accurately measured by the compassionate actions of its members." - Coretta Scott King

Note:These quotes reflect the importance of compassion, respect, and the moral treatment of animals, emphasizing their role as companions and sentient beings deserving of care and consideration.
