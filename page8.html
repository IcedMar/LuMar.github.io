7. EDUCATION

Education is the process of facilitating learning, acquiring knowledge, skills, values, beliefs, and habits through various methods such as teaching, training, research, and discussion.
It is important because it empowers individuals with critical thinking skills, enhances their ability to solve problems, fosters personal growth, and contributes to the overall development of society by promoting informed citizenship and economic prosperity.

Quotes

Here are five quotes about education:
1. "Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world." — Nelson Mandela
2. "The function of education is to teach one to think intensively and to think critically. Intelligence plus character—that is the goal of true education." — Martin Luther King Jr.
3. "Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire." — William Butler Yeats
4. "The roots of education are bitter, but the fruit is sweet." — Aristotle
5. "Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today." — Malcolm X
